About Me

Hi my name is Charlie. I am a developer in training at the UNC Chapel Hill Coding Bootcamp. I recieved my Bachelor's Degree in Geology from NC State in 2016 but after working in the industry for a few years, I realized my passion was elsewhere. I decided to pursue software developoment as a career because I have always loved technology, solving problems and creating things!

I have proven skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript/jQuery, API Documentation/JSON/AJAX, NodeJS and more! If you would like to check out some of my work, please click on the projects link where you can view all the projects I completed to assisted in development.

As a developer in a mobile-first world, I strive for excellence in creating attractive, intuative and mobile-first applications that can be accessed on any device. My expertise is in front end web-develpment working with HTML, CSS and JavaScript however I do enjoy dabbling in server side work such as NodeJS. In my free time I have always enjoyed outdoor activities and exploring new places. In the spring and fall, I go camping, mountain biking, sport shooting, and hiking. In the winter time I try to spend as much time snow skiing as possible because winter is my favorite season. In the summer I find myself out on the lake, water skiing and knee-boarding.

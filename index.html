About Me

Hi my name is Charlie. I am a full-stack web developer leveraging a Bachelor's degree in geology from North Carolina State University and a certification in full-stack web development from the University of North Carolina Chapel Hill. I am passionate about solving complex problems, learning new things and developing beautiful applications with a focus on UX/UI design and development. Excited to leverage skills and provide fresh ideas as part of a passionate, continually learning, quality-driven team in order to build better experiences on the web.

I have proven skills in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript/jQuery, React, Handlebars, Node, Express, MySQL, MongoDB, MVC Design, Mobile Responsiveness and Agile Development Environments! If you would like to check out some of my work, please click on the projects link where you can view all the projects I completed or assisted with.

As a developer in a mobile-first world, I strive for excellence in creating attractive, intuative and mobile-first applications that can be accessed on any device. I have experience in both front-end and back-end development environments. I enjoy creating beautiful front-end experiences in React and Bootstrap as well as creating clean server-side code in Node and Express. In my free time you can find me tinkering with my car, exploring the outdoors or gaming!
